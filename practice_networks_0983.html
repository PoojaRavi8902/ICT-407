C) Structured response practice (exam style)

Write answers using “because…” and link to the scenario. Use the mark scheme after you attempt.
Q1 (6 marks): A school is expanding to a new building nearby. Explain whether a LAN, WLAN, or WAN is most suitable for connecting computers and printers, and justify your choice.
Show mark scheme (after attempting)
  • Choose LAN if same site; WAN if across distant sites; WLAN if mobility needed (1–2)
  • Justify with area size, speed, ownership, cost, reliability (2–3)
  • Mention at least one drawback + mitigation (e.g., security → WPA2/WPA3, access control) (1–2)
Q2 (6 marks): Explain why a switch is preferred to a hub on a busy office network.
Show mark scheme (after attempting)
  • Switch forwards to correct device/port using MAC tables (2)
  • Reduces congestion/collisions compared to hub broadcasting (2)
  • Improves security because traffic isn’t sent to all devices (2)
Q3 (8 marks): A company stores staff documents in the cloud. Give four advantages and four disadvantages.
Show mark scheme (after attempting)
  • Advantages: access anywhere, collaboration, scalability, backups, reduced local storage (any 4 with reasons)
  • Disadvantages: internet dependency, security/privacy, subscription cost, outages/vendor lock-in (any 4 with reasons)
Q4 (4 marks): Explain what DNS does and why it is needed.
Show mark scheme (after attempting)
  • DNS converts domain names to IP addresses (2)
  • Needed because humans remember names but routing uses IP numbers (2)
